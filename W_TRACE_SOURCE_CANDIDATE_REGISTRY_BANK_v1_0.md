# W_TRACE Source-Candidate Registry Bank v1.0

This v11.5 layer banks the external-source acquisition checklist and source-candidate registry for future W_TRACE finite-part payloads.

No real finite-part rows are admitted in this release. Candidate entries are acquisition targets only: they do not contain numerical finite-part values, they do not certify a component sum, and they do not unlock the physical W/on-shell export path.

The registry requires any future candidate source to document: source class, locator, revision/version, license or access note, extraction method, extraction-log digest, payload digest plan, review attestation, forbidden-input audit, scheme/gauge policy, and counterterm-convention alignment.

Forbidden inputs include observed M_W, W world-average data, W residuals, APF-anchor Delta r target values, component-sum residuals to the APF-anchor, post-hoc counterterm fits, and any physical export request. The APF-anchor comparison target remains comparison-only and cannot enter acquisition or payload construction.

physical W export remains locked until real independent rows, a component-sum certificate, covariance certification, uncertainty propagation, counterterm convention, and final export readiness all pass together.
