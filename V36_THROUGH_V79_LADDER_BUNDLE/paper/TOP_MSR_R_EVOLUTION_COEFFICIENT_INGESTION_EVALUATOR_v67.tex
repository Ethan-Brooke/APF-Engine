\label{sec:v67-top-msr-r-evolution-numeric}

\paragraph{Purpose.}
The v66 gate required the external $R$-evolution coefficient table.  This v67 module ingests the $R$-anomalous-dimension coefficient family, implements a numerical $R$-evolution evaluator, and keeps the claim in the MSR codomain.

\subsection{Evaluator}
The implemented equation is
\[
  \frac{d}{d\ln R}m_t^{\rm MSR}(R)
  =-R\sum_{n=0}^{L-1}\gamma_n^R
  \left(\frac{\alpha_s(R)}{4\pi}\right)^{n+1},
\]
or equivalently
\[
  m_t^{\rm MSR}(R_{\rm EW})
  =m_t^{\rm MSR}(R_\star)+
  \int_{R_{\rm EW}}^{R_\star}
  dR\,\gamma_R[\alpha_s(R)].
\]
The path does not cross a flavor threshold, so v67 uses fixed $n_\ell=5$.
