
\section{Charged-Lepton Generation Residual Closeout}\label{sec:charged-lepton-residual-closeout}

The charged-lepton trace vector admits a common APF-native six-channel normalization,
\begin{equation}
  \lambda_6=\frac{1}{\sqrt{6}},
\end{equation}
but the normalized vector does not yet define a pole-mass export.  Applied to the APF trace entries, it gives
\begin{equation}
  (m_e,m_\mu,m_\tau)_{\lambda_6}
  =(0.526765004,\ 106.728287257,\ 1705.453337061)\ {\rm MeV}.
\end{equation}
Against the charged-lepton pole masses, the residuals are
\begin{equation}
  (+3.085\%,\ +1.013\%,\ -4.022\%).
\end{equation}
Thus the universal normalization captures the common lepton scale only to percent accuracy and cannot be promoted to a physical pole export.

The residual geometry is itself informative.  The APF trace ratios give a Koide parameter
\begin{equation}
  Q_{\rm APF}=0.661348767976,
\end{equation}
compared with
\begin{equation}
  Q_{\rm pole}=0.666664463415,
  \qquad Q_{\rm Koide}=\frac{2}{3}.
\end{equation}
Equivalently, the APF square-root mass vector lies at
\begin{equation}
  \theta_{\rm APF}=44.769640129^\circ
\end{equation}
relative to the democratic direction, whereas the pole vector lies at
\begin{equation}
  \theta_{\rm pole}=44.999905322^\circ.
\end{equation}
This shows that the APF charged-lepton trace vector is close to the physical shape geometry, but not coincident with it.

We therefore close the charged-lepton sector at the following status:
\begin{equation}
  (e,\mu,\tau)^{\rm APF\text{-}TRACE}:
  [P_{\rm trace\ vector}+P_{\rm normalization\ candidate}+P_{\rm generation\ residual\ closeout}],
\end{equation}
while explicitly not claiming
\begin{equation}
  (e,\mu,\tau)^{\rm APF\to pole}:[P_{\rm export\ candidate}].
\end{equation}
The first failed export gate is
\begin{equation}
  \texttt{APF\_CHARGED\_LEPTON\_GENERATION\_RESIDUAL\_OPERATOR\_DERIVATION}.
\end{equation}
A future export theorem must derive the residual generation operator independently of the pole masses.  Any exact three-generation reconstruction from observed pole masses is quarantined as target-fitted and is not counted as proof.
